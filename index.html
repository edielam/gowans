About Us

Gowans Lab focuses on exploring the Genetic Architecture of Dental, Oral, and Craniofacial Phenotypes.

We investigate craniofacial malformations' genetic causes, with a particular focus on populations of African ancestry, aiming to enhance understanding and therapeutic approaches.

Learn More

Research Areas & Interests

Explore our diverse research interests and areas.

Genetic Factors in Orofacial Clefts

Investigating genetic and environmental influences in the etiology of human orofacial clefts.

Molecular Genetics of Craniosynostosis

Understanding the molecular genetics underlying craniosynostosis for improved diagnosis and treatment.

Genetic Risk Factors for Alzheimer's Disease in African Populations

Exploring genetic predispositions to Alzheimer's disease among African populations.

Gene Therapy and Molecular Therapeutics

Developing gene therapy and targeted molecular therapeutics for craniofacial disorders.
